## 🔄 **Example: Complete Email-to-Action Flow**

```
Email arrives: "Invoice #1234 for $5,000 due Nov 15"

1. GmailParserAgent (Layer 1)
   └─> Extracts: {sender, subject, invoice_amount: $5000, due_date: "Nov 15"}

2. PersonRecognitionAgent (Layer 2)
   └─> Recognizes: {John Smith, CEO, Company X, trusted_vendor: true}

3. CompanyRecognitionAgent (Layer 2)
   └─> Recognizes: {Company X, industry: Tech, relationship: vendor}

4. FinancialAnalystAgent (Layer 3)
   └─> Analyzes: {urgency: medium, cash_flow_impact: low, recommendation: approve}

5. InvoiceWorkflowAgent (Layer 4)
   └─> Orchestrates: [create_task, schedule_payment, update_quickbooks]

6. LearningAgent (Layer 5)
   └─> Learns: "John's invoices always < $10k, always on-time"
   └─> Creates rule: "Auto-approve John < $10k"

Next time: Auto-approved in 2 seconds! 🚀
```
